What makes a hero?

A hero is someone that we look up to. Heroes embody what we truly value most. Therefore who we select as role models says a lot about who we are as people.

My mom has given up so much to allow my brothers and me to have the best life possible. She is someone who has a kind heart, but also protects the people she loves the most. I am grateful I see parts of her in my personality.
